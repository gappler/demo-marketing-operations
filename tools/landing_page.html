Materials

What sailcloth nylon actually means

Most packs use 500D or 1000D Cordura. It's fine. It works. It wears out.

1680D sailcloth nylon is built for sustained load, abrasion, and UV exposure in conditions that would shred standard pack fabric in a season. We didn't over-engineer this. We just didn't cut the corners most companies cut.

The result is a pack body that ages the way good gear should — slowly, and with character.
