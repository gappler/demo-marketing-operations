Most packs use 500D or 1000D Cordura. It's fine. It works. It wears out.

The Basecamp uses 1680D sailcloth nylon — the same material used in commercial rigging and marine sails.

It costs us 3x more than the industry standard. We use it anyway.

The result: a pack body that's abrasion-resistant, field-repairable, and built to age the way good gear should — slowly, and with character.
